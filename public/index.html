What is the
Break The Shackles about?

IEEE BPIT Student Branch brings you a unique opportunity to unleash your creativity with Break The Shackles 3.0. A hackathon-like-no-other, choose from 9 unique tracks that you can revolutionize with your skills. Interact with like-minded individuals as you hack your way up the leaderboard, going toe-to-toe with the best in your field as you leave your mark on the world of innovation. Innovate, inspire and work towards making your ideas a reality and win lucrative prizes and SWAGs as you prove yourself to the world throughout a 24-hour spree of a pure frenzy.

Tracks

Renewable & Affordable Energy
Renewable & Affordable Energy
Energy advancement refers to how energy is used — and not used — in the manufactured state to improve environmental and human advantages. Cloud computing is a rapidly evolving field that uses next-generation technologies to deliver cutting-edge services to users and companies all over the world.
Waste Management
Waste Management
The activities and actions necessary to manage trash from its inception to its final disposal are referred to as waste management (or waste disposal). This includes garbage collection, transportation, treatment, and removal, as well as waste management measure monitoring and guidance. Waste management can be accomplished through Automated Waste Collection, Route Optimization, and other methods.
Fin-Tech
Fin-Tech
Fintech is a blend of the words "finance" and "technology," and it refers to any company that employs technology to improve or automate financial services and operations. The word refers to a large and rapidly growing industry that caters to both customers and businesses.
Agriculture & Rural Development
Agriculture & Rural Development
To alleviate poverty and hunger, agricultural and rural development is essential. Crop irrigation systems may be monitored and controlled by a smartphone, Crop Sensor, Satellite Imaging, Weather tracking which is a good example of how modern technology can be used to better agriculture.
HealthCare & Biomedical devices
HealthCare & Biomedical devices
Health services have the ability to analyze network medical conditions, identify unmet needs and assess the assets available to address them, set SMART goals, and expand managerial actions to achieve the goals of suggested activity programs.
ICT, Cyber-physical systems, Blockchain
ICT, Cyber-physical systems, Blockchain
The infrastructure and components that enable modern computing are known as ICT (information and communications technology). The phrase is widely understood to refer to all devices, networking components, applications, and systems that work together to allow people and businesses to connect digitally. Smart grids, autonomous automobiles, medical monitoring, industrial control systems, are all examples of cyber-physical systems (CPS).
IoT based Technologies
IoT based Technologies
The Internet of Things (IoT) is a network of physical objects—"things"—that are equipped with sensors, programming, and other advancements to connect and exchange data with other devices and frameworks through the internet. Smart home security systems, wireless inventory trackers, ultra-high-speed wireless internet, biometric cybersecurity scanners, and other IOT devices are examples.
Cloud Computing, AI & ML
Cloud Computing, AI & ML
Cloud computing is the distribution of computing services via the Internet (the cloud), including servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, and intelligence, in order to provide faster innovation, more flexible resources, and economies of scale. AI entails a machine performing a task that a human could perform. Machine learning is when a machine learns from its previous experiences and makes judgments based on that knowledge.
Open Innovation
Open Innovation
If your idea does not fall in any of the track you are free to build it under this track.

Frequently Asked Questions

When are Registrations beginning?

Applications will out on 18th Nov 2021.

What are the Application Deadlines?

6th Nov 2021 is the deadline for the Participants' Applications.

What if I cross the deadline? Can I apply even after deadlines?

If you cross the deadline, you are not able to apply for this year’s program. But hold your breath and wait for next year.

What is Discord?

Discord is a communication platform where you can communicate with your mentors and clear your doubts as well.

Who all are eligible to participate?

Anybody who is willing to learn new skills and willing to mentor the participants are eligible to participate.

Is there any application/registration fee?

No, it is free for all.

Is there any age limit for participation?

No, there is no age limit. Anybody can apply for this program before the deadlines.

Is this only for technical domain people?

This program is for both technical and non-technical areas.

What are the prizes and perks for winning?

Certificates will be provided to your registered mail ID.

What minimum conditions should the project satisfy?

This will be decided by your Mentor/Team Leader/Moderator based on their knowledge.

When will the results publish?

Results will be published on 9th November.

As a beginner, from where do we start to contribute in the projects?

Knowing and assuming you as a beginner, the mentor will help you throughout the project.

How will I know about my selection?

You will get mail from us, If you get selected.

How will I and my team communicate with my mentors?

You can communicate through discord channels and can clear your doubts by messaging, calling or video conference calling.

How long will this program be active?

The program starts on 8th November and ends on 9th November. All the best.
